Undergraduate Researcher, Sep 2020 - Jun 2021

I utilised the classical approaches of Proportional Navigation and Acceleration Velocity Obstacles for doing so. In parallel, I pursued courses in data science and machine learning, building a sound foundation in optimization and decision-making algorithms.
