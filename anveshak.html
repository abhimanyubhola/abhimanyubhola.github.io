Team Anveshak, IIT Madras

Mechanical Team Member, Feb 2019 - May 2020

I was a part of Team Anveshak, the student Mars rover team of IIT Madras, during the first two years of my college life at IIT Madras. This was my first experience of working on a hands-on project in a team, and we spent countless nights working on the design, manufacturing and assembly of the rover.

I primarily worked on the development of the wheel and the on board science laboratory for the third generation of the rover. This experience allowed me to appreciate the trade-offs between theoretical design and manufacturability, while exposing me to the sheer amount of work that goes into the development of the smallest of components.

Eventually, we would compete and finish fifth in the Indian Rover Challenge 2020, and qualify for the finals of the University Rover Challenge 2020.
