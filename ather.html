Ather Energy, Bengaluru

Battery Engineering Intern, Dec 2021 - May 2022

I undertook an internship in the battery engineering department at Ather Energy in my pre-final year, which was my first exposure to the automotive industry and its challenges. My work included the implementation of laser welding for cell to busbar linkages in battery packs, where I tested materials and fine-tuned process parameters to meet pull force and resistance specifications. Ensuring precise weld placement across hundreds of cells proved challenging here, and this introduced me to the critical role of tolerances and precision in mass manufacturing. To tackle this issue, I worked on the design a jig which ensured consistent busbar to cell contact, and provided guidance to the laser for accurate weld positions.

I was also involved in the research and analysis of battery pack imbalance in the on-field Ather 450X scooters. As a part of this project, I designed experiments alongside the cell engineering team to do the RCA of the these issues. I also built Python tools to analyse the field the field data to analyse and group the field data into multiple issues.
