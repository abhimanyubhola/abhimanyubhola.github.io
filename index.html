about

I am Abhimanyu, a 2023 graduate from the Engineering Design department of IIT Madras, with a specialization in Automotive Engineering. I am passionate about the design, manufacturing and overall functioning of precision mechanical hardware, especially focussing on microscopic mechanisms and the machines that make them.

I have experience working as a design engineer for Inito, a health tech startup, where I designed and productionized the imaging system for a wireless lateral flow assay reader. In college, I developed a reinforcement learning based fast charging algorithm as part of my thesis project. I also participated in numerous extracurricular activities during my time at IITM, building rovers, developing obstacle-avoidance algorithms, and even serving as the department legislator!

My work has always been driven by the principle that I want to be happy with whatever I am doing, and to always give my 100% in every endeavour. Feel free to reach out to me if you want to discuss ideas involving mechanical hardware, control systems, or anything tech-related.
